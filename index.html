Scale-Up Seasons 2026 — Roadmap

Four quarterly seasons · each anchored by 3 Tea On events + 1 paid MasterBuild workshop
Strategic rationale: Scale-Up Seasons serve two goals simultaneously. First, they clarify F2's positioning in the ecosystem — this is a community for scale-ups, not early-stage startups. Second, they act as a sales funnel: The Tea On events introduce founders to F2's thinking and community, creating a natural path into the paid quarterly MasterBuild workshops.

✅ What is working well

The rooms are full — session attendance in 2026 is on par with 2025. Companies are sending whole teams (up to 14 people per company), creating richer group dynamics. The programme is cohesive, with high-quality speakers including Afonso Santos (Kevel), Inês de Macedo Santos (Armilar VC) and Tiago Craveiro (Maersk). Codepoint — a 2025 participant — signed up again in 2026. That's the clearest product-market signal possible.

⚠️ Point to acknowledge: Board Dinners

Board Dinners (reserved for founders and C-Level) are less attended this year, simply because there are fewer companies in the programme. With 6 companies vs 15, the dinner table is smaller by design. The format is valuable — the challenge is having sufficient volume of eligible companies to make it work at full energy.

🔴 The challenge: the recruitment funnel

Of 44 leads captured, only 6 converted. Pipeline analysis shows most leads were early-stage and international startups — outside the programme's target (Portuguese, later-stage companies). This mismatch explains the low conversion rate. The strategic question for 2027: how do we qualify leads earlier and more precisely before they enter the funnel?

🔵 Bottom line

BBBB 2026 is stronger and more cohesive than the raw numbers suggest. The challenge is the recruitment funnel, not the quality of execution. The 2027 edition should start working the pipeline much earlier, with clearer fit criteria around stage, geography and business model.

Klatchpoint — BBBB Communications

All BBBB programme communications run through Klatchpoint — built by BBBB alumni — replacing scattered emails with a single automated system.

📧

Unified Communications

Emails and push notifications centralised in one platform. Participants receive all programme communications in a single channel — no more scattered inboxes or missed threads.

📝

Slides & Session Summaries with Notion AI

After each session, slides are shared and an AI-generated summary is produced via Notion AI — accessible to all participants immediately after the session ends.

📸

Photos Ready to Download

Session photos are available for instant download through the platform — no manual sharing via email or WeTransfer required.

📅

Automatic Google Calendar Invites

Calendar invites for each session are sent automatically by the app — eliminating the manual process and ensuring every participant has events in their calendar from day one.

🎓

Automatic Certificates

Completion certificates are generated automatically at the end of the programme — personalised per participant and sent without any manual effort from the F2 team.

🔗

Built by BBBB Alumni

Klatchpoint was built by former BBBB participants — a concrete example of the programme creating value that flows back into the community. F2 is simultaneously customer and proof of concept.

Alumni Product
Operational impact: Adopting Klatchpoint significantly reduced the administrative overhead of running the programme — fewer manual emails, fewer calendar follow-ups, less photo management. Time recovered was reinvested into content quality and speaker curation.
